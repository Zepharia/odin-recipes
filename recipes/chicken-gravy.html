Chicken Gravy

Description

This recipe is for simple chicken gravy. The total time to make it is 15 minutes. It's made by drippings and its quick and delicious to make.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 cup drippings from a roast chicken
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients
  2. Place flour in a medium bowl
  3. Pour 2 to 3 tablespoons into the flour and whisk until combined and thick, but not pasty.
  4. Transfer both liquid and flour mixtures to a roasting pan set over a burner on the stove.
  5. Whisk to combine and turn heat to medium. Cook, stirring constantly, until gravy is thickened and bubbly. Season with salt and pepper.
